# Wedge Break Reversal Playbook

## Summary
A reversal playbook where a grinding move compresses into a wedge-like structure, momentum and participation deteriorate, and the break back out of the wedge creates the reversal opportunity.

## Status
Draft

## Playbook family
Reversal

## Brand
The grind exhausts itself, the wedge traps late participants, and the break flips the move.

## Context

### Regime
- lower participation grind
- mature intraday move
- slowing directional pressure

### Event types
- none
- post-news fade or cleanup
- slower session expression after an initial move

### Session structure
- often strongest on 1m to 5m intraday compression after a move into a reference

### Higher timeframe conditions
- stronger when the wedge forms into a meaningful destination or stretched location
- stronger when the larger move already looks mature

### Structural references
- wedge boundary
- recent grind channel
- nearby profile reference
- destination level or stretched extension point

### Participation conditions
- usually not a straight trend day with strong broad participation
- often better when directional pressure is getting tired

## Playbook definition

### Preceding conditions
- sustained grind in one direction
- repeated pushes produce less clean follow-through
- market compresses and becomes vulnerable to reversal

### Mechanics
1. directional grind forms wedge/compression
2. signs of weakening appear
3. break out of wedge occurs against prior move
4. reversal confirms and can expand quickly

### Identifier
- compression shape
- delta divergence, slowing pace, or failure to push cleanly
- inability to extend despite repeated attempts

### Confirmation
- break of wedge structure
- improved pace in reversal direction
- inability of original move to reclaim the wedge cleanly

### Expected progression
- initial break can be quick
- continuation depends on whether the reversal is just a scalp or phase one of bigger reversal
- best cases expand once trapped participants unwind

### Target logic
- opposite side of recent intraday structure
- prior balance / profile reference
- larger reversal objective if context supports phase expansion

### Invalidation
- break fails immediately and wedge is reclaimed
- original direction continues with clean pace
- compression was not exhaustion, only pause

### Failure modes
- seeing wedge everywhere
- fading a strong trend day too early
- taking first crack at reversal before actual break/confirmation

### Execution notes
- can support aggressive access on early shift and conservative access on break
- context determines whether this is scalp-only or core reversal candidate
