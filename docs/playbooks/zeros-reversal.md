# Zeros Reversal Playbook

## Summary
A reversal playbook associated with exhausted continuation, finished or unresponsive auction behavior, and a reversal opportunity once the market can no longer progress through the expected zone.

## Status
Draft

## Playbook family
Exhaustion / reversal

## Brand
The move should keep going, but instead it goes to zero and flips.

## Context

### Regime
- exhausted move
- mature directional leg
- reversal after continuation quality collapses

### Event types
- none
- post-event continuation failures

### Session structure
- intraday pattern with higher value when linked to larger structure or destination

### Higher timeframe conditions
- stronger when move is already stretched or at a destination
- stronger when continuation logic was obvious and then fails

### Structural references
- exhaustion zone
- finished auction area
- destination level
- prior extension objective

### Participation conditions
- original move must have enough prior energy that failure matters
- strongest when late continuation traders are vulnerable

## Playbook definition

### Preceding conditions
- move has already traveled and participants expect more continuation
- signs emerge that continuation is drying up or no longer attracting healthy follow-through

### Mechanics
1. move extends into expected continuation zone
2. continuation quality deteriorates
3. market cannot keep progressing
4. reversal begins as continuation thesis dies

### Identifier
- expected continuation does not show up cleanly
- auction appears finished or unresponsive
- inability to gain further ground despite favorable setup

### Confirmation
- reversal-side pace improves
- continuation-side attempts fail repeatedly
- key local threshold is reclaimed against original move

### Expected progression
- reversal can begin subtly and then strengthen once continuation players exit
- best cases produce clean unwind from finished auction area

### Target logic
- unwind toward prior accepted area
- revisit prior structural pivot
- larger reversal if exhaustion aligned with destination context

### Invalidation
- continuation reasserts and pushes cleanly through the zone
- reversal cannot reclaim meaningful local threshold

### Failure modes
- fading every pause in trend
- calling something finished when it is only pausing
- lacking a clear expectation for what should have happened next

### Execution notes
- requires clarity about expected continuation behavior
- stronger when paired with larger structure and participation context

## Translation

### Source markets
- futures, especially where continuation exhaustion is visible in profile / OF

### Transferable to
- broad-market stocks and liquid names if continuation expectation is clear

### Constraints
- depends on knowing what should have happened if trend continuation were healthy
